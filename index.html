About Our Community

Nestled in the heart of The Village, Oklahoma, the Villas at the Vineyard offers a unique blend of urban convenience and serene seclusion. Our gated community of 60 exquisite homes is just north of prestigious Nichols Hills, minutes from Lake Hefner, Casady Square, and premier shopping destinations.
